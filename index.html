Biography

Jinpeng Yu (于劲鹏) is currently a researcher at Xiaohongshu AIGC team, working primarily on controllable and conditional content generation, as well as multi-modality understanding and generation. He obtained his BS with honors from HIT and Master's degree in SIST from ShanghaiTech University, supervised by Prof. Shenghua Gao (高盛华).
